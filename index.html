About Me

For the past eleven years, I have aligned my education and professional skills toward creating user experiences that are inviting, empowering, and true to values espoused by the organizations for which I have worked.

Hello There! I'm Chris Harris

As a full stack web developer, I am skilled in both front-end and back-end web development. On the front-end, I am proficient in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript and can create dynamic, responsive user interfaces using frameworks such as React, Angular, or Vue. On the back-end, I have expertise in one or more programming languages such as Python, Ruby, or Java, and am familiar with databases and server-side frameworks like Node.js, Django, or Flask.

I have a strong understanding of web development concepts and can architect and develop scalable, secure, and maintainable web applications. I am also experienced in version control, testing, and deployment, and can collaborate effectively with designers, product managers, and other developers to bring complex web projects to life.
